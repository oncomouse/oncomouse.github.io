#modsource

The Open Access, Agile, Modernist
Source Book

#modsource is an archive of modernist source files—poems, short stories, articles—compiled by a group of volunteers for use in the instruction of and research into modernist literature.

Our documents are listed below. Feel free to use any of them and share them as you see fit. The source code is also available on GitHub.
